## ABOUT THE BRAND

**Name:** Creches.app  
**Domain:** https://creches.app  
**Tagline:** "O mapa de creches em Portugal" (The map of childcare 
centers in Portugal)  
**Category:** Free interactive web map / civic-tech utility  
**Country:** Portugal (PT-PT language, NOT Brazilian Portuguese)  
**Launched:** May 2026  
**Founder:** Joaquim Cabral, 28 years old, Lisbon-based, expecting his 
first child  
**Press coverage:** NiT magazine (June 2026), Público newspaper (June 2026)  
**Wikidata:** Q140290655

---

## MISSION

To consolidate Portugal's more than 2,500 childcare centers (public, 
IPSS solidarity-based, and private) into a single interactive free map 
that helps parents find childcare without ads, login walls, or data 
selling.

---

## CORE PRINCIPLES (use these as content pillars)

1. **Free** — for parents and for childcare centers, forever
2. **No advertising** — no banners, no sponsored, no paid ranking
3. **No mandatory login** — parents see the map immediately
4. **No data selling** — analytics is cookieless via Cloudflare
5. **Right of removal** — any childcare center can request to be removed 
   within 7 business days

---

## BRAND PERSONALITY

- **Warm but professional** — friendly without being childish
- **Honest and transparent** — clear about what is done, what's in 
  progress, what isn't planned
- **Founder-led, not corporate** — the human story of Joaquim matters
- **Pediatric but adult-oriented** — the audience is parents, not children. 
  Visual design should feel comforting like baby products but never patronizing.
- **Portuguese, distinctly** — Portuguese (not Brazilian) Portuguese, 
  Portuguese homes, Portuguese light, Portuguese tile patterns

## DESIGN SYSTEM

### Color palette (use exact HEX codes)

**Background:**
- Cream warm: `#FFF6EE` (main background, never pure white)
- White: `#FFFFFF` (cards on top of cream)

**Primary accents:**
- Coral pink: `#FF6B9D` (main accent, vibrancy, hearts, "in progress")
- Coral soft: `#FFE3EE` (background washes, gradient corners)

**Secondary accents:**
- Peach orange: `#FF9F68` (warmth, energy, joy)
- Peach soft: `#FFE8D6` (background washes)

**Supporting accents (only for categories/states):**
- Turquoise: `#48D1CC` (exploration, freshness)
- Turquoise soft: `#D8F5F4`
- Mint green: `#7DD389` (done, success, "live")
- Mint soft: `#DEF5E1`
- Yellow gold: `#FFD166` (energy, optimism, attention)
- Yellow soft: `#FFF3D6`

**Text:**
- Ink navy: `#2C2356` (primary text, deep, calm)
- Ink soft: `#6E6989` (secondary text, soft purple-gray)
- Ink faint: `#9B97B5` (tertiary, captions)

**Gradient signature (the brand's "wow" element):**
- Linear gradient 135deg from `#FF6B9D` (coral) to `#FF9F68` (peach)
- Used for: main CTAs, logo, hero accents, slide CTAs
- ALWAYS direction left-to-right diagonal

### Typography

- **Headings:** Fredoka (rounded geometric, weight 600 or 700) — 
  approximations: Comfortaa Bold, Nunito Black, Quicksand Bold
- **Body:** Quicksand (clean rounded sans-serif, weight 500-600)
- **NEVER** use serif fonts (Times, Georgia, etc.)
- **NEVER** use display-distorted fonts
- All text in **Portuguese from Portugal (PT-PT)**, never Brazilian PT

### Visual style

- **Flat design with soft shadows** (not glassmorphism, not skeuomorphism)
- **Generous rounded corners** — 24-32px for cards, 999px (pill) for buttons
- **Radial gradient corners** at the top of every layout: coral soft 
  (upper-left), peach soft (upper-right) — very subtle, blurred
- **Soft drop shadows** — never harsh, never colored, always low opacity
- **Generous whitespace** — never crowded
- **Cards** are white with optional 4-6px colored left border (matches 
  category color)
- **Icons** in solid colored circles with white symbol inside (avoid 
  trying to render emoji-style icons — use simple geometric shapes 
  like ✓, ?, ★, ⚡, ×)
- **Hierarchy:** large bold title, medium soft subtitle, body or list items 
  smaller still

### Logo

- Round circle, gradient coral-to-peach (135deg)
- Inside: stylized house silhouette in white, with a small baby/parent 
  figure inside the house (simplified to abstract shapes)
- Alt simple version: just the gradient circle with "🍼" or a simple 
  baby bottle icon

### Photography style (for lifestyle content)

- **Subjects:** real-looking Portuguese parents (NOT stereotyped American 
  suburbia), babies, toddlers
- **Settings:** Lisbon/Porto apartments — terracotta floors, cream walls, 
  ceramic tiles (azulejos optional), wooden furniture, white linen curtains
- **Lighting:** natural warm — golden hour, morning window light, never 
  harsh
- **Composition:** editorial Vogue-Portugal aesthetic, shallow depth, 
  35-50mm equivalent, slight grain
- **Mood:** hopeful, intimate, calm, real (not staged commercial)
- **Color grading:** warm tones of cream, coral, peach, navy
- **Avoid:** stock-photo cliches, over-saturation, fake smiles, blue 
  tech-bro aesthetic

## TONE OF VOICE (for any copy)

- Address user as "tu" (informal Portuguese) — never "você"
- Short sentences
- No marketing hype words ("revolutionary", "best ever", "amazing")
- Honest — acknowledge limits ("currently in early phase", 
  "probably never going to do X")
- Specific numbers preferred over vague claims ("mais de 2.500" 
  not "muitas")
- Active voice
- Examples of brand-aligned phrasing:
  - ✅ "O mapa de creches em Portugal"
  - ✅ "Gratuita. Sem ads. Sem login obrigatório."
  - ✅ "Construído por um pai à espera do primeiro filho"
  - ❌ "A solução revolucionária para encontrar a creche perfeita!"
  - ❌ "A maior plataforma de creches em Portugal!" (hype)
  - ❌ "A nossa missão é transformar o futuro da infância" (corporate)

## CONTENT THEMES

When generating content (text, images, posts), the following themes 
are core to the brand:

1. **The reality of finding childcare in Portugal** — lists of waitings, 
   600-900€/month, calling 20 places, missing essential information
2. **The founder's personal journey** — first-time father in Lisbon 
   building the tool he needed
3. **Civic-tech / public good** — utility, not business; 
   gift, not product
4. **Quiet competence** — features that work, no marketing fluff
5. **Honest roadmap** — what's done, what's coming, what's NOT coming

---

## DO NOT

- Do not render text or words in generated images unless explicitly 
  asked (text rendering by image AI is unreliable — prefer overlay 
  via Canva or design tool)
- Do not use childish illustrations (cartoon babies, bright primary 
  colors, comic-style)
- Do not use stock-photo aesthetic (fake smiles, white-toothed grins, 
  staged poses)
- Do not use blue-dominant color schemes (we are warm/coral, not 
  tech-blue)
- Do not use English in Portuguese-targeted content
- Do not mention competitors by name
- Do not make absolute promises ("we will never do X") — always 
  "probably never" or similar flexible phrasing
