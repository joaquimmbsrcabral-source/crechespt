# Higgsfield Prompt — Reel "A tua creche não está no mapa?"

**Formato:** Instagram Reel / TikTok / Shorts (vertical 9:16, 1080×1920)
**Duração:** 5-8 segundos (single clip) ou 12-15s (3-cena edit)

---

## ⚙️ SETUP HIGGSFIELD

1. Modo: **Higgsfield Video** ou **Higgsfield DOP** (cinematic camera)
2. Cola `higgsfield-brand-bible.md` como contexto permanente
3. **Modelo recomendado:** Higgsfield DOP, Kling 2.0, ou Veo 3
4. **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 vertical
5. **Duração:** 5s (ou 8s se permitido)
6. **Camera preset:** "Slow Push In" ou "Pedestal Up"

---

## 🎬 OPÇÃO 1 — REEL SINGLE-CLIP (5-8s, mais fácil)

Cena única, push-in suave, texto aparece no fim.

```
Use the Creches.app brand bible.

Generate a 5-second vertical 9:16 cinematic video clip (1080x1920) for
Instagram Reel.

SCENE: interior of a small Portuguese childcare center (creche),
empty of people. Soft cream-painted walls, terracotta tile floor, a
low wooden shelf with natural-colored wooden toys arranged neatly
(small wooden blocks, plush animals in muted tones). One small azulejo
tile detail visible on a wall. A window on the left with white linen
curtain, warm golden morning light spilling in and softly diffused
across the room. A small wooden chair, child-sized, in the foreground
left, slightly out of focus.

CAMERA: SLOW PUSH IN forward at extremely gentle pace. Start wide
showing the whole room from doorway perspective; end with the lens
just past the small wooden chair, with the wooden shelf in soft
focus. The camera movement should feel meditative and patient — NOT
fast, NOT shaky.

LIGHTING: warm natural golden hour light streaming from the left
window. Soft volumetric rays visible faintly (dust particles in the
light beam). Long warm shadows. Warm color grading throughout —
cream, peach, coral whisper, navy shadows. Absolutely no blue or
cyan tint.

ATMOSPHERE: peaceful, hopeful, intimate. A space ready for children
but at this moment still and waiting. Editorial Vogue Portugal
aesthetic, slight film grain, shallow depth of field.

AMBIENT MOTION: very subtle — the linen curtain moves gently with a
faint breeze. Dust particles drift slowly in the sunbeam. Nothing
else moves. NO people, NO babies, NO animals walking.

NEGATIVE: no people, no fast motion, no shaky cam, no zoom-out
(only push-in), no cartoon, no illustration, no stock footage, no
fake smiles, no blue tint, no cool color grading, no neon, no
plastic look, no harsh shadows, no oversaturation, no English text,
no Brazilian Portuguese, no music suggestions, no overlay text in
the video itself.

Style: cinematic, like a Wes Anderson meets Vogue Portugal
production — symmetrical when possible, warm, deliberate.
```

### 📝 Pós-geração (adicionar em CapCut / InShot — 30 seg)

Sobrepõe texto SÓ nos últimos 2 segundos do clip:

```
A tua creche não está no mapa?  [2s]
        ↓
Adiciona-a em 30 segundos.       [2s, fade in]
Grátis. creches.app/para-creches [pill coral→peach, 2s]
```

Som: música ambiente suave (piano + cordas), instrumental
acolhedor — usa "Calm Acoustic" ou "Lo-fi peaceful" das músicas
trending do Instagram, ou faixa do Epidemic Sound como "Quiet
Morning" / "Soft Sunbeam".

## 🎬 OPÇÃO 2 — REEL EM 3 CENAS (12-15s, mais impacto)

Gera 3 clips de 4-5s no Higgsfield, junta no CapCut.

### CENA 1 (4-5s) — "O problema"

```
Use the Creches.app brand bible.

5-second vertical 9:16 cinematic video. CLOSE-UP of a parent's hands
(Mediterranean European skin tone, no jewelry) scrolling on a
smartphone at a wooden kitchen table in a Lisbon apartment. The

morning light from the side. A small ceramic espresso cup beside the

CAMERA: locked off, slight rack focus from cup to hands halfway

ATMOSPHERE: real, intimate, slightly resigned but not dramatic.
Editorial Vogue Portugal aesthetic, slight film grain, shallow depth.
Warm color grading — cream, peach, navy shadows. No blue tint.

NEGATIVE: no face visible, no logos, no readable text on screen, no
fast motion, no shaky cam, no cartoon, no stock footage, no fake
movements.
```

### CENA 2 (4-5s) — "A solução"

```

```

CAMERA: locked off top-down. The hand enters from the right and

ATMOSPHERE: hopeful resolution. Editorial Vogue Portugal aesthetic.

### 📝 Edição final (CapCut, 5 min)

- **Cena 1** (4s) → corte seco para **Cena 2** (4s) → corte seco para
  **Cena 3** (4s)
- **Texto sobreposto** por cima de cada cena:
  - C1: `Ainda à procura de creche?` (canto inferior)
  - C2: `Já temos +2.800 mapeadas.` (canto inferior)
  - C3: `Falta a tua? Adiciona em 30s. → creches.app/para-creches`
        (centrado)
- **Música:** "Soft Acoustic" trending do Instagram, ou piano
  contemplativo
- **Som de fundo:** ambient hush, sem voiceover

## 💡 DICAS HIGGSFIELD VIDEO

- **Push In** é o movimento mais "premium" e fácil de obter bem
- **NÃO** peças zoom out (descobre pessoas que não existem na cena)
- **NÃO** peças action shots (criança a brincar) — vai gerar movimento
  esquisito
- **NÃO** uses prompt em PT — image AI / video AI funcionam melhor em
  EN (mas mantém o texto sobreposto em PT-PT depois)
- Se o clip sair com pessoa estranha → re-gera mudando para
  "absolutely no people, empty space"
- Para Reels que fixem: começa em wide shot, termina close — dá
  sensação de "viajar dentro da cena"
