# Higgsfield Prompt — "A tua creche não está no mapa?"

**Conceito:** Post de Instagram a chamar creches que ainda não estão no mapa
para se adicionarem via `creches.app/para-creches`.

**Formato:** 1080×1080 (Instagram square feed)

---

## SETUP NO HIGGSFIELD

1. Cola o ficheiro `higgsfield-brand-bible.md` como contexto permanente
   (Computer / Super Computer)
2. Depois usa o prompt abaixo

---

## 🎯 PROMPT PRINCIPAL (Concept A — Recomendado)

**Cena:** Educadora portuguesa num espaço de creche.

```
Use the Creches.app brand bible.

Generate a 1080x1080 photorealistic editorial photograph for Instagram feed.

Subject: a Portuguese woman in her late 30s, kindergarten/creche
educator, wearing a soft cream-colored linen shirt or apron, warm smile
but not posed — caught mid-thought. Natural skin, no heavy makeup,
realistic and approachable. NOT model-styled, NOT American-suburban-mom.
She has medium-length dark brown hair, gentle eyes, looks Mediterranean
European.

Setting: interior of a small Portuguese childcare center (creche).
Cream-painted walls, terracotta tile floor, a low wooden shelf with
small baskets of toys (wooden blocks, plush animals — soft natural
colors, NOT bright primary plastic). One azulejo tile detail in
background, blurred. Window with white linen curtain letting in soft
morning golden light from the side.

She is holding a smartphone in one hand, looking at the screen with
gentle curiosity — as if she just discovered something useful. Phone
screen is not visible / out of frame.

Composition: editorial portrait, 35mm equivalent, shallow depth of
field, focus on her face and the phone, soft bokeh in background. She
is positioned slightly off-center (rule of thirds). Plenty of negative
space on the upper-left and right side of frame for text overlay later.

Lighting: warm natural golden hour from the side, soft, cinematic,
slight grain. Color grading: warm tones — cream, peach, coral whisper,
deep navy shadows. Avoid blue/cyan tints.

Mood: calm, hopeful, professional but warm — like a Vogue Portugal
editorial about Portuguese family life. NOT staged commercial photo,
NOT corporate stock photo, NOT cartoon, NOT illustration.

No text, no logos, no graphics in the image — just the photograph.
Vertical safe areas left for text overlay in Canva later.
```

## 🎯 PROMPT ALTERNATIVO (Concept B — Mapa físico)

**Cena:** Detail shot — mãos sobre mapa de Portugal com pinos.

```
Use the Creches.app brand bible.

Generate a 1080x1080 photorealistic top-down photograph for Instagram
feed.

Subject: a top-down (bird's eye) view of a wooden Portuguese rustic
table. On the table: a cream-paper map of Portugal (hand-drawn,
illustrated, abstract — not a real cartographic map) with small coral
pink and peach orange pins scattered across most regions. ONE area of
the map has a visible empty patch with NO pins — slightly highlighted
by a hand pointing to it with one finger (only the hand and forearm
visible, Mediterranean European skin tone, no jewelry).

Around the map: a ceramic Portuguese coffee cup (espresso), a small
azulejo coaster, a moleskine-style notebook open to a blank page, a
pencil. Composition is editorial Vogue, balanced.

Lighting: warm morning natural light from upper-left, soft shadows
falling lower-right. Warm cinematic color grading — cream paper,
coral/peach pins, terracotta wood, navy notebook. Avoid blue/cyan.

Style: editorial overhead shot, magazine-quality, slight film grain,
not stock-photo. Negative space at top-right for text overlay.

No text on the map, no logos, no graphics. Just the photograph.
```

## 📝 NOTAS PARA O HIGGSFIELD

- **Aspect ratio:** 1:1 (1080×1080)
- **Style preset:** "Editorial photograph" ou "Cinematic"
- **Negative prompt:** `cartoon, illustration, anime, 3D render, stock
  photo, fake smile, perfect teeth, plastic skin, blue tint, cool color
  grading, watermark, text, logo, graphic, distorted face, blurry
  subject, neon, glossy, oversaturated`
- **Reference / mood:** Vogue Portugal editorial, A Magazine, Kinfolk —
  warm, real, hopeful, Mediterranean European
- **Variations:** gera 3-4 e escolhe a com expressão mais natural
